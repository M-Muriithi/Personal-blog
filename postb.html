A Quiet Beginning

Posted on May 7, 2025

It’s quiet here — and that’s exactly how I wanted it.

This blog begins not with a bang, but with a breath. A soft place to rest my thoughts. A still garden for words that have waited patiently to bloom.

I don’t know how often I’ll write. I don’t know who will read. But I know this: something stirs when we make space for what matters. When we speak, even if our voice is small.

So here I am. Writing. Beginning.

And that’s more than enough.

– Nonnie
